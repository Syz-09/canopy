About CANOPY

We document how Stanford students build—with consent, context, and clarity. Here’s how we work and what we care about.

1

Submit

You share a project or nominate one using a short structured template. No long forms—just enough to get the story started.

2

Edit for clarity

Editors shape the draft so it’s legible beyond insider context. We keep your voice and add structure.

3

Consent & publish

Nothing goes live without your confirmation. Once published, profiles stay revisitable and can be updated as the project evolves.
